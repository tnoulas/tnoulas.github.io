BiographyJohn

Since 2020 I am Head of Data Science at Firefly leading the development of our mobility analytics and measurement platform. In this context I am contributing to the strategic development of a metrics-driven client-relevant product roadmap. At the same time I am directing our efforts in building an effective and sustainable ecosystem of data partners that fosters accelerated revenue growth for our company. I completed my PhD in 2013 in the Network and Operating Systems group at the Computer Laboratory in the University of Cambridge. My main research interests include human mobility modeling, social network analysis, location-based services and disruptive technologies deployed in the urban space. During my career in scientific research I published at top-tier conferences and journals in the fields of machine learning, online social systems and user behavior modeling, combining artificial intelligence and complex systems techniques. In the past I have worked as a Data Scientist at Foursquare Labs in New York and Telefonica Research in Madrid. I have advised a number of Start Ups in the geo-location and mobility tech space. Part of my energy is devoted towards the development of open-source and society-relevant projects. In 2015 he has teamed up with researchers in Cambridge and Belgium to launch the OpenStreetCab project whereas since 2019 I am contributing to the development of the Wiki Atlas project, a collaboration with the Wikipedia Research Foundation and the University of Torino. I have also served as an Assistant Professor and a Faculty Fellow at the Center of Data Science, New York University between 2017-2020 introducing the Wiki Atlas project.
